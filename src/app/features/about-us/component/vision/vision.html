Our Vision

To make learning the Quran and Arabic accessible for everyone around the world through modern, flexible, and interactive online education.

To make Quran and Islamic learning accessible to everyone around the world.

Our Mission

To spread the knowledge of the Quran and Arabic language in an engaging, high-quality, and authentic way — through qualified teachers certified by Al-Azhar Al-Sharif, and a system that supports both learners and their families.

To provide engaging online Quran and Islamic classes with certified teachers.

Our Values

At Quran For All School, we value sincerity and dedication in our work, authenticity in the knowledge we share, and respect for every learner regardless of age, gender, or background.

We believe in lifelong learning, inclusivity, and continuous development — striving to make every learning experience meaningful, spiritual, and empowering.

We believe in teaching with sincerity, clarity, patience, and care for every learner.
