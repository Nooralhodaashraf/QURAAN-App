About Quran For All School

Quran For All School helps students worldwide learn Quran and Islamic studies through interactive online classes with certified teachers.

  • Quran For All School is an online educational platform that teaches the Holy Quran and the Arabic language to learners of all ages, genders, and nationalities — anytime and anywhere.
  • We believe every Muslim deserves the opportunity to understand, recite, and live by the words of Allah, while connecting deeply with the Arabic language, the language of the Quran.
